How

Our approach is to help our client through this process - "the 5 D's".

Our defined process ensures that we are always aligned with the clientâ€™s needs.

Discover
Data

Discover

Before resolution comes consultation. We want to understand your company, your needs, and help you reach your goals. Depending on your requirements, we hold day-long Inspiration Days or weeklong Design Sprints to reach the solution best fitted for your needs.

Data

Once we understand your needs, our next step is to use your step files, 3D models, data sets, and other such content in order to create a prototype. This prototype is then fine-tuned until it satisfies your goals and needs.

Alternatively, if the solution does not require an elaborate prototype, this is where we start designing.

Design

With the prototype and materials ready, we take the next step to designing the solution. Here we create the storyboard and discuss branding options along with UI and UX opportunities.

Develop

With all the basics covered, the next step is to start developing the solution. Here we use programs like Unity, Javascript, Maya among others to create solutions that are applications, websites, virtual reality programs, etc.

In the case of 3D animation, this is where we start going back and forth with different versions.

Deliver

After showcasing small parts of the solution over time, the final solution is handed over to the client in the preferred technology, i.e. as an application on an iPad, installed in an Occulus, available on mobiles, etc.
