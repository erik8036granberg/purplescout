GeoPark Odsherred

AR/VR-experience

AR / VR experience universe for Geopark Odsherred

Purple Scout aided GeoPark Odsherred with an AR / VR experience universe. They helped created an app which used augmented reality (AR) and a 3D experience universe to let the user travel thousands of years back in time and view how the area looked during 4 different ages.

Purple Scout created this AR / VR experience universe as an interactive realtime 3D experience on smartphones and tablets. They integrated this with overview maps to guide the user to other cultural experiences in the area.
