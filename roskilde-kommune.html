Roskilde Kommune

AR-app

Purple Scout aided Roskilde Kommune in a campaign that showcased the libraries' digital offerings via augmented reality. For this, they used a unique AR experience with a hologram effect. This shows a giant smartphone on the floor where newspapers and magazines are pouring out and allows the consumer to discover and see how large their digital selection is. This is currently an interactive screen installation at the libraries in the Roskilde Municipality.

Purple Scout helped with the production of the AR experience for the event space, integrated the library database so that it is always the most recent newspapers and magazines displayed, established a physical event space in the libraries to attract attention and produced teaser movies, as a screensaver on other libraries' screens.
