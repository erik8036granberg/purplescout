UI/UX, Graphics and Experience Design

At Purple Scout, graphic design is as much a matter of effortless visual communication as the aesthetics of a website or app. We care about the quality of experience and scope of use you want to give your customers.

App, mobile development and digital solutions

We love creating solutions that will be a joy for your customer to use. We help you identify the real needs of your users and create applications, websites and other digital solutions that make a difference to both parties.

Film, 3D animation and motion graphics

Moving pictures are often the easiest way to explain a complex subject. We work with both 2D and 3D animation, film, editing and post-production. Through close dialog, together we will find the best solutions aimed at the right target audience.

AR/VR/MR

Purple Scout works with digital technologies such as AR and VR to provide businesses with innovative and fun ways of delivering information to their customers. In the future, these solutions will not only open brand new worlds but also provide us with fun ways to increase customer engagement. It will save businesses billions of dollars in the coming years - from reconstructing travel and lifestyle to energy consumption cuts.

Cloud Solutions and AWS partner

AI and machine learning

To succeed with AI requires an understanding of what works as well as what is likely to go wrong. At Purple Scout, we help you to start using your data not only to explain what has already happened but to make educated guesses about what will happen in the future.
